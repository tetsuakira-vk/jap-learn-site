N3 is the bridge between beginner and intermediate. You'll need ~3,750 words and solid grammar including conditionals, passive, causative, and complex sentence structures.

## What to expect at N3

- Reading: Short articles, menus, signs, simple news
- Listening: Everyday conversation at natural speed
- Grammar: て-form compounds, conditionals (たら/ば/と/なら), passive/causative
- Kanji: ~650 characters
