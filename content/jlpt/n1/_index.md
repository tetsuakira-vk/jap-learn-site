N1 is the highest JLPT level. Passing it demonstrates near-native reading and listening ability. Most of the 10,000+ words at this level are found in literature, academic writing, and specialist fields.

## What to expect at N1

- Reading: Literature, academic papers, legal/business documents
- Listening: Abstract discussion, rapid speech, regional accents
- Grammar: Highly formal patterns, classical Japanese elements, four-character compounds (四字熟語)
- Kanji: ~2,000+ characters

## Sample N1 vocabulary

| Japanese | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 曖昧 | aimai | ambiguous / vague |
| 恣意的 | shiiteki | arbitrary |
| 逡巡 | shunjun | hesitation |
| 敷衍 | fuen | elaboration / expansion |
| 翻弄 | honrou | to toss about / manipulate |
