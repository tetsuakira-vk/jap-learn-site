N2 is the level required for most Japanese university admissions and many jobs in Japan. You'll need ~6,000 words, confident reading of newspapers, and advanced grammar patterns.

## What to expect at N2

- Reading: Newspapers, essays, longer articles, technical texts
- Listening: TV news, lectures, nuanced conversation
- Grammar: Complex formal structures, indirect speech, nominalisation
- Kanji: ~1,000 characters

## Sample N2 vocabulary

| Japanese | Reading | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| 義務 | gimu | obligation / duty |
| 矛盾 | mujun | contradiction |
