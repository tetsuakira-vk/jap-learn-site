## Animal Sounds 動物の鳴き声

Japanese animals make very different sounds from their English counterparts!

| Animal | Japanese sound | Romaji | English sound |
|--------|----------------|--------|---------------|
| 犬 (dog) | ワンワン | wan wan | woof woof |
| 猫 (cat) | ニャーニャー | nyā nyā | meow |
| 牛 (cow) | モーモー | mō mō | moo |
| 豚 (pig) | ブーブー | bū bū | oink |
| 鳥 (bird) | チュンチュン | chun chun | tweet |
| 蛙 (frog) | ケロケロ | kero kero | ribbit |

> **Cultural note:** The fox (狐 *kitsune*) and raccoon dog (狸 *tanuki*) are magical shapeshifters in Japanese folklore. You'll see tanuki statues outside restaurants across Japan — they're considered good luck symbols.
