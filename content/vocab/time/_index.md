## Telling the Time 時刻

Add **〜時** (*-ji*) for the hour and **〜分** (*-fun/pun*) for minutes.

<table class="kanji-table">
<thead><tr><th>Japanese</th><th>Reading</th><th>Romaji</th><th>English</th></tr></thead>
<tbody>
<tr><td><span class="kt-char" onclick="speak('いちじ')">1時</span></td><td>いちじ</td><td>ichi-ji</td><td>1 o'clock</td></tr>
<tr><td><span class="kt-char" onclick="speak('さんじ')">3時</span></td><td>さんじ</td><td>san-ji</td><td>3 o'clock</td></tr>
<tr><td><span class="kt-char" onclick="speak('くじ')">9時</span></td><td>くじ</td><td>ku-ji</td><td>9 o'clock</td></tr>
<tr><td><span class="kt-char" onclick="speak('じゅうにじ')">12時</span></td><td>じゅうにじ</td><td>juuni-ji</td><td>12 o'clock</td></tr>
<tr><td><span class="kt-char" onclick="speak('さんじじゅっぷん')">3時10分</span></td><td>さんじじゅっぷん</td><td>san-ji juppun</td><td>3:10</td></tr>
<tr><td><span class="kt-char" onclick="speak('ごじはん')">5時半</span></td><td>ごじはん</td><td>go-ji han</td><td>5:30 (half past 5)</td></tr>
<tr><td><span class="kt-char" onclick="speak('なんじですか')">何時ですか？</span></td><td>なんじですか</td><td>nanji desu ka?</td><td>What time is it?</td></tr>
</tbody>
</table>

> **Tip:** 半 (*han*) means half — so 3時半 = 3:30. 〜時ごろ (*-ji goro*) means "around [time]" — useful for approximate times.
