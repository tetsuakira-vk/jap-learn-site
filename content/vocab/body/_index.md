## Useful Phrases 便利な表現

| Japanese | Romaji | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 頭が痛い | atama ga itai | I have a headache |
| お腹が痛い | onaka ga itai | My stomach hurts |
| 足が痛い | ashi ga itai | My leg/foot hurts |
| ここが痛い | koko ga itai | It hurts here |

> **Note:** 足 (*ashi*) covers both "leg" and "foot" in Japanese — context makes clear which is meant. Similarly, 手 (*te*) can mean "hand" or "arm" depending on context.
