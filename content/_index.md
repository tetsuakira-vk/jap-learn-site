## How this site works

Each section has two parts: a **reference chart** you can click to hear pronunciations, and an **interactive quiz** to test yourself. No sign-up needed — just dive in.

**Recommended order:**

1. Read [Start Here](/start/) to understand the writing systems
2. Learn [Hiragana](/hiragana/) — use the chart, then take the quiz daily
3. Move to [Katakana](/katakana/) once hiragana clicks
4. Start picking up [Kanji](/kanji/) alongside the YouTube lessons
5. Practice real [Phrases](/phrases/) as soon as possible

> Click any character on the reference charts to hear it pronounced.
